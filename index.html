Our Units

Specialized teams for every aspect of public safety.

Education & Training Unit

The Education & Training Unit is responsible for ensuring that all officers in the department are properly trained in police procedures and roleplay guidelines. They help new recruits become familiar with department policies and ensure experienced officers stay updated on protocols.

Special Weapons & Tactics

The SWAT is an elite team within the department that handles high-risk operations, such as hostage situations, armed robberies, and barricaded suspects. They are equipped with advanced weaponry and tactical gear. And even fly with helicopters!

Strategic Protection Unit

The Strategic Protection Unit is responsible for safeguarding high-profile individuals, including the Chief Command and VIPs. The Strategic Protection Unit is the only Unit who is allowed to guard the gate! They also handle security at major events to prevent threats. Overall they are protection the academy.

Transport Control Unit

The Transport Control Unit is responsible for maintaining order on the roads and waterways, overseeing transport, and ensuring public and official transportation safety. They enforce traffic and maritime laws while also assisting in high-speed pursuits and emergency escorts.
